# The Dream Cybersecurity Course


After completing the course, the student will:


Theme: Identifying Cybersecurity Risks and Scams
- Be able to recognize cybersecurity development areas within their own organization, identify scams and risks, and point out improvement suggestions and areas that require special attention.


Theme: How the Internet Works & How Attackers Exploit It
- Understand how the internet works and how attackers use it to carry out their operations.


Theme: Protecting Devices and Environments
- Know how to secure workplace and home devices against attacks and how to identify ongoing security risks.


Theme: Safe Online Behavior
- Be able to act safely online.
- Recognize and internalize safe online behavior and apply it in the workplace and organizational culture.
- Understand phishing attacks and attacks targeting the organization.
- Understand the big picture of cybersecurity and know how to protect themselves online.


Theme: Cybersecurity Frameworks and Certification Skills
(This also connects to the learning approach described below.)
- Learn the basics of industry certifications.
- NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)
- Katakri
- ITIL 4 Security Management — all students complete the certification.



# Learning Approach


- The school would provide access to industry certifications and external courses to support the curriculum. Students who wish to do so could certify their skills during the course and continue learning for up to six months afterward.
- Personalized learning paths based on each student’s job role.
- Guest lecturers or presentations from experienced professionals or advanced students, possibly including school alumni.
- No group work. Ever.

# Hands-On Practical Labs


Phishing Detection Lab
- Identify scams and social engineering attempts.
- Deepfakes and AI-driven attacks.
- Practice with phishing emails sent by the instructor during the course. The links lead to a safe, instructor-built training site.


Password Lab (From User and Attacker Perspectives)
- How to create a strong password and what makes a password strong.
- Practice cracking passwords.


Wireshark Lab
- Use Wireshark in a controlled environment.
- Learn how data moves across a network.


Building a Secure Home Network
- Learn how to secure personal devices and accounts.
- Understand how to build a secure network environment at home.
